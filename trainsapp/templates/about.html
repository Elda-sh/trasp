In the last decade, everyone that entered the forest had come out with severe memory loss: forgetting where they lived, who their family members were etc.

Phil watched helplessly as the shadows of trees engulfed him. He knew he had gone down the correct route, yet somehow the train had led him to this area. The motion in his body started to move forward; the train was slowing down. "That's not right" Phil thought. Something was very wrong. As the train came to a halt, Phil went to grab his torch, walking past the communication equipment that had given him nothing but silence for the past hour. He began to head for the door and froze.
